Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really a one-time payment?
Yes! You pay once and get access to all current and future Premium features forever. No hidden fees or recurring subscriptions.
Can I switch from Free to Premium later?
Absolutely. You can start with the Free plan to see if FloatBoard fits your workflow, and upgrade directly from inside the app whenever you hit the limits.
What payment methods do you accept?
We use PayPal as our secure payment processor. You can pay with your PayPal balance, or use any major credit/debit card through PayPal even if you don't have an account.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If FloatBoard Premium isn't right for you, just let us know within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
How do I activate Premium?
After your purchase via PayPal, your unique License Key will be sent to your PayPal email address within 24 hours. Simply open FloatBoard, go to settings, click 'Upgrade', and paste your key to instantly unlock all features.
